# Free Tier

Fused is accessible to anyone for free by simply creating an account! Head over to [Workbench](https://www.fused.io/workbench) and sign up!

## Features

You get access to:
- Daily compute credits
- Access to [AI Assistant](/workbench/ai-assistant/)
- Saving UDFs & creating [shared tokens](/guide/data-input-outputs/export-api/tokens-endpoints)

:::important Free Tier works on shared compute

All users on the free tier share the same compute resources.
Do not share sensitive data that others might be able to access on the free tier. 

:::

Upgrade to a [higher tier](https://www.fused.io/pricing) to get your own dedicated environment & compute resources.
