The Integrations & Secrets page is where you connect third-party services to Fused and store the keys your UDFs need at runtime.

![Integrations & Secrets page](/img/workbench/integrations-secrets/integrations-secrets.png)

## Integrations

Connect external services (like Slack and Snowflake) to Fused so canvases and UDFs can interact with them. See the [Slack integration guide](/workbench/integrations/slack) and the [Snowflake integration guide](/workbench/integrations/snowflake) for setup details.

## Secrets

Store any keys or credentials you want to use in Workbench here. They're encrypted in the Fused backend and attached to your selected kernel. For details on managing secrets, see the [Secrets management](/guide/advanced-setup/secrets-management) guide.

To use a stored secret from a UDF, see [`fused.secrets`](/python-sdk/api-reference/fused-secrets).
