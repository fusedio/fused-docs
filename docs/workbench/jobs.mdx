 The Jobs page displays all [dedicated jobs](/guide/working-with-udfs/udf-best-practices/scaling-out#dedicated-instances) executions, organised into: 
- Personal Jobs: Jobs you started 
- Team Jobs: Any job started by your team 

Each job includes detailed information such as Logs, Metrics, Code, and Status History, allowing you to monitor and review execution results.
